# Life Insurance Policies

## Basic Term Life
- Coverage: $100,000
- Premium: $20/month
- Term: 20 years
- Notes: Provides basic life insurance coverage for individuals.

## Standard Term Life
- Coverage: $500,000
- Premium: $50/month
- Term: 30 years
- Notes: Offers more coverage with a longer term.

## Whole Life Premium
- Coverage: $1,000,000
- Premium: $180/month
- Term: Lifetime
- Notes: Lifetime coverage with additional benefits for policyholders.
